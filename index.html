Comp Sci Cafe

Our Mission

Comp Sci Cafe seeks to create a more equitable community, bringing awareness to the struggles of sleep-deprived CS students throughout the Chapel Hill/Carrboro area.

About Us

We strive to create a calming and welcoming environment to all of our stressed out students and customers. We are located 4th floor of Sitterson Hall. Stop by for coffee, baked goods, and a study break! This location features indoor and outdoor seating, with high-speed wifi available.
